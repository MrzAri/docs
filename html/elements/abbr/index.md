|Content=The '''abbr''' element is a type of phrasing content that exists at the sub-paragraph level. It may not contain block-level elements. 

The <code>title</code> attribute may be used to provide an expansion of the abbreviation or acronym. The attribute, if specified, must contain an expansion of the abbreviation, and nothing else.
